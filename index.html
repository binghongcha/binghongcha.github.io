ABOUT ME

I have extensive experience designing products in multidisciplinary teams, from inception to launch. My work lies at the intersection of user centered design, digital craft and design strategy.

I am passionate about designing products people use everyday, improving them by iteration and learning through failure. I love to craft visual languages and branded experiences, to fight for efficient solutions though proper user experience.

During my career I’ve also focused on data visualisation, specifically on the use of communication design as a means to approach, understand and effectively resolve complex problems.
